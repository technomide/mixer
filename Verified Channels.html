Creating an Account

Vibestream.tv is a part of the Microsoft family of products, in order to create a Vibestream.tv account you must first have a Microsoft account.

Creating a VibeStream.tv account via web

Visit VibeStream.Tv
Click "sign in" in the top right Click "create account"
This will open a new pop up prompting you to sign into your Vibe account. Enter the details and you are ready to Vibe With your audience.
If you would like to change your VSTV username, you may do this via your account page once every 10 days.

Creating a Vibestream.tv account via Xbox One

If you already use an Xbox One, creating a VCTV account is as simple as opening the VCTV app on Xbox. We'll generate you a VCTV account using your Xbox Live credentials. You can then start using your normal VCTV login to sign into VCTV on available platforms.
When you like to change your VCTV username, you may do this via your account page once every 10 days. Now that you have a VCTV account, why not setup your account in more detail or begin streaming!
