Benefits

Streaming is a journey where you share your adventures and build a community. As your community grows you'll unlock new features to share with them.

Embers

Viewers support aspiring streamers through Embers, a paid virtual currency. Embers are used for skills such as animated or holographic stickers, or even on-screen effects!

Subscriptions (coming soon)

Viewers can support aspiring streamers by subscribing to them. Once subscribed, they earn a unique badge and emotes!

Customised Skills (coming soon)

Viewers can support streamers by using customised Ember skills unique to the channel.
